Description

As a little child, Miyo Sasaki strayed into an unknown world and, scared and surrounded by cat-like beings, started to cry, when a large cat reached out its paw and asked her with a smile, whether she was lost. This was the first time she met this mysterious, big cat. Many years pass and she is annoyed with her parents because she must decide which of the two she wants to live with from now on. So, she runts into a forest and once more meets the big cat, which, this time, is surrounded by cat masks, and asks Miyo whether she wouldn’t like to try one on herself. On this day, Miyo’s life is completely turned on its head, as she can now transform into a white cat called Tarou!

Miyo has a crush on her classmate Kento Hinode, but she constantly annoys him with her extroverted attitude. Every day after school, but also whenever she has private problems, she visits him in her cat form, to be close to him, and thereby gets to know new sides to him, also realising that she prefers her life as a cat to her life as a human. Thus, she decides to leave her true form behind, and the lines between her human and her cat form become more and more blurred, which is not without consequences for her …
