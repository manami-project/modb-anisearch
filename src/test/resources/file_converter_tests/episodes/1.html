Description

Shouya Ishida is the class clown and has already made a name for himself among his fellow students and teachers. When the hearing-impaired Shouko Nishimiya comes to his school, it doesn’t take long until he and his group make her out as a new victim for their bullying and increasingly brutal pranks. When another one of Shoukos expensive Cochlea implants “goes missing,” her mother finally demands that something has to be done. Due to the severity of the bullying and the costs for the implants, the teachers and the school administration are in uproar, and a culprit is soon found in Shouya. To escape the bullying, Shouko switches schools again and Shouya, now left and betrayed by his former cronies, is left on his own while he is now the victim of all bullying.

So he grows up, alone and with nothing but hatred for all strangers and those who so willingly stabbed him in the back. But he learned from his mistakes and now tries to make amends for his past misdeeds. He works part-time after school to repay his mother for the costs of Shouko’s implants and he taught himself sign language to officially ask Shouko for forgiveness. The road towards redemption, however, is long and hard – especially if you have never really forgiven yourself …
