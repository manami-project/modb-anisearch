Anime Description

“Bakugan: Battle Planet” enters its fourth round and brings back Dan Kuusou, his friends Shun Kazami, Wynton Styles, Lia Venegas and their dog Lightning! Due to strange circumstances, the Bakugan get too much energy and now rampage through the area. At the same time, however, this brings an advantage for the heroes because their Bakugan begin to evolve through the elemental energy of Vestroia – new fighters are created who take “Bakugan Brawling” to a whole new level! Can Dan and his friends, together with their Bakugan partners, continue to endure …?
